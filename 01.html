Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, the protagonist of the Japanese manga 'One Piece' and its derived works. Nicknamed 'Straw Hat' Luffy, he is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, and one of the Worst Generation. A Rubber Fruit ability user, with a bounty of 1.5 billion Berries. His dream is to find the legendary One Piece and become the Pirate King.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, a character in the Japanese manga 'One Piece' and its derived works. He is a fighter in the 'Straw Hat Pirates', also known as the 'Pirate Hunter'. He is one of the 11 'Supernovas' who arrived at the Sabaody Archipelago two years ago. He is also one of those known as 'The Worst Generation'.

Sanji

Sanji, a character in the Japanese manga 'One Piece' and its derived works. He is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, known as 'Black Leg' by the navy due to his precise and ruthless kicking techniques. His bounty is 330 million Berries. A gentleman among pirates, he has curly eyebrows, half of his face is always covered, he is always seen with a cigarette in his mouth, he loves women, is fickle but very chivalrous, and never wishes to harm any woman, even if she is an enemy.
